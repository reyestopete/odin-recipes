How to make a Martini

Desription

The Martini is the perfect evening cocktail. Good gin with a just a little bit of complexity added, served in a classy stemmed glass.

Ingredients (the way I like it)

A good, dry gin (I prefer something with as much fruity or floral notes as possible). Dry vermouth. Orange bitters.

  • 2.5 oz dry gin
  • 1 oz dry vermouth
  • 2 dashes orange bitters

Steps

  1. Add gin, vermouth, and bitters to a mixing glass
  2. Stir for 30 seconds or until properly diluted
  3. Strain into chilled cocktail coupe or martini glass
  4. express zest of a lemon over cocktail and garnish with lemon peel
  5. drink and enjoy
