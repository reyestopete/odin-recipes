How to make a Daiquri

What is a Daiquiri?

You may think of a frozen strawberry daiquiri or a big plastic cup from fat tuesdays on the Las Vegas strip but for this recipe we are looking at a classic Daiquiri. A daiquiri is simply good rum, sugar, and fresh lime juice.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum (I prefer a french rhum, the funkier the better)
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • .75 oz simple syrup

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass
  2. Fill mixing glass with ice
  3. Stir for 30 seconds or until properly diluted
  4. Double strain into a chilled cocktail coupe
  5. Express lime peel over glass and garnish with either fresh lime peel or dried lime
  6. Drink and enjoy
