How to make a Negroni

Desription

The Negroni perfectly balances the floral notes of Gin with a bitter italian aperitivo and sweet vermouth. It's an even ratio of all three ingredients with options for small tweaks for the drinkers preference.

Ingredients

There is some flexibility with the ingredients here. Pick your dry gin of choice. The standard recipe calls for campari but feel free to use any other bitter you enjoy, I prefer Forthave Spirit's Red Aperitivo. All sweet vermouths taste slightly different but accomplish the same goal. Use whatever you like or have access to. Try not to use the cheapest bottle though.

  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Bitter
  • 1 oz Sweet Vermouth

Steps

  1. Add gin, bitter, and vermouth to your mixing glass
  2. Fill the mixing glass nearly to the top with ice
  3. Stir the ingredients in the glass for 30 seconds or until properly diluted
  4. Strain into a chilled coupe
  5. Express an orange peel over top and add as a garnish
